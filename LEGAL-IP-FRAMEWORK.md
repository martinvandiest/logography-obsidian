## What We CANNOT Do

### 1. Distribute Transcripts

- Cannot put transcripts in public GitHub repos
- Cannot include transcript content in the product
- Cannot share transcripts with beta testers or collaborators
- Cannot train a model on transcripts and serve that model commercially

**Why**: ND clause prohibits derivative works. Distribution violates both ND and NC.

### 2. Use Pierre's Name/Identity in Product Without License

- Cannot call it "The Pierre App"
- Cannot represent the AI as Pierre Grimes or as speaking for him
- Cannot use his likeness, credentials, or reputation in marketing
- Cannot claim endorsement or affiliation with the Noetic Society

**Why**: Right of Publicity (CA Civil Code § 3344). Trademark. Common law publicity rights.

### 3. Quote Lecture Content in Product

- Cannot put Pierre's exact words into the AI's responses
- Cannot include lecture excerpts in the app, help docs, or marketing
- Cannot reproduce the "To Artemis" questions verbatim in the product

**Why**: Specific expression is copyrightable even when the method is not.

### 4. Assume Archive.org = Public Domain

- Availability ≠ license
- CC-BY-NC-ND is the most restrictive CC license
- "NonCommercial" and "NoDerivatives" are clear prohibitions
- Some items may have different or no licenses — verify individually

## The "Logos Extraction" Research Strategy

### What It Is

Using AI to analyze the transcribed lecture corpus for structural patterns of how insight (the logos) emerges during dialectical sessions. This is original research — we're discovering principles that Pierre practiced intuitively but never codified as a system.

### What We're Looking For

- **Emotional arcs** that precede breakthrough moments
- **Question sequences** that reliably surface false beliefs
- **Resistance patterns** and how they resolve
- **Timing** — how long before insight typically emerges
- **Language shifts** — how the dreamer's vocabulary changes before/after insight
- **Conditions for deflation** — what makes a belief let go vs. hold on
- **Recursion depth** — how deep the layers typically go

### How It Makes the Product Better

Instead of "AI that talks like Pierre," we get "AI that understands the *structure* of insight." The product can:

- Recognize when the user is approaching a breakthrough (based on pattern matching)
- Adjust questioning strategy based on where the user is in the insight arc
- Identify resistance patterns and respond appropriately
- Track the depth of exploration and know when to push vs. when to pause

### Why It's Legally Clean

- We're analyzing patterns, not copying words
- The findings are statistical/structural, not expressional
- The output is our original research, published nowhere
- The product contains our insights, not Pierre's transcripts
- Attribution can be given voluntarily in marketing ("inspired by decades of dream analysis research")
