# Dream Journal / Analysis App Market Research
**Date:** April 21, 2026

## Market Overview
The dream journal/analysis app market is a niche but growing segment within broader mental health/wellness/journaling apps.

### Market Size Estimates
- Journaling apps overall: ~$2-3B globally (2025)
- Dream-specific apps: ~$150-300M globally
- Lucid dreaming hardware + software: ~$50-100M
- Market is fragmented with many small indie developers

## Major Competitors

### Tier 1: Established Apps

**1. Journey (journey.cloud)**
- Downloads: 5M+ (Android), 94K reviews, 4.3★
- General journaling app with dream AI (Odyssey AI)
- Platforms: iOS/Android/Mac/Windows/Web
- Pricing: ~$4-5/month or ~$30-40/year (premium)
- Features: Multimedia, mood tracking, E2E encryption, coach programs, PDF/DOCX export, self-hosting option

**2. DreamApp (DreamApp Ltd)**
- Downloads: 1M+, 4.0★ (12.5K reviews)
- Dream interpretation & journal
- Pricing: ~$60/year
- Features: AI interpretation (OpenAI/ChatGPT), dream dictionary, live therapist chat
- Differentiator: Therapist connection

**3. Dream Catcher (Snaps Studio AB)**
- Downloads: 100K+, 4.2★ (8K reviews)
- Dedicated dream journal
- Pricing: ~$6.99 one-time (Pro), ads in free
- Features: Tags/emotions, pattern analysis, lucid tools, cloud sync, PIN lock

### Tier 2: Specialized Dream Apps

**4. Lucidity (Christopher Benz)**
- Downloads: 100K+, 4.2★ (7K reviews)
- Lucid dreaming focused with AI
- Monetization: Freemium + IAP + ads
- Features: AI interpretation, lucid tutorials (WBTB/WILD/MILD/SSILD), reality checks, charts, PDF export, offline-first/privacy-first

**5. Oniri (Oniri Inc)**
- Downloads: 10K+, 3.0★
- Dream journal + interpretation + lucid dreams
- Monetization: Premium subscription (monthly & yearly)
- Features: Voice recording, AI interpretation, sleep lab techniques, image generation, PDF export

**6. Dream Journal Ultimate (Dream Labs LLC)**
- Downloads: 100K+, 4.2-4.3★
- Social dream journal with "Dream Wall"
- Monetization: Freemium + IAP
- Features: Social sharing, AI imagery/interpretation, cross-device sync, symbol dictionary

**7. Temenos Dream**
- 4.5★ rating, Jungian psychology focus
- Features: Voice transcription, AI interpretation, Dream Circles, encryption

### Tier 3: Smaller Apps
- LucidMe: 100K+ downloads, 3.6★, hardware (~$200 mask)
- Elsewhere: 5K downloads, 4.2★, AI imagery, research-oriented
- Dreamboard: Free, research/data focus (since 2013)
- Others: Dreamy (4.5★), Dreamly (4.6★), Dream Advisor (4.6★), DreamMuse (4.5★)

## Key Trends
1. AI interpretation now standard (OpenAI/ChatGPT integration)
2. AI dream image generation as premium feature
3. Privacy as differentiator (offline-first)
4. Community/social features emerging
5. Lucid dreaming tools common
6. Multi-platform expected
7. Hardware integration growing
